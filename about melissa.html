Facts About Me

I am a wife and a mother.

I am also a police officer with the City of Miami Police Department, of which I have been employed for 19 years.

I was first introduced to grahic design when I was in the 7th grade.

While there, I developed a love for writing. I quickly went from being a campus news reporter to assistant editor.
